About Us

Welcome to Harchive, a healthtech startup driven by a vibrant team of engineering students from ABU Zaria. Our mission is to address critical challenges in Nigeria's healthcare system through innovative deep-tech solutions. By creating a suite of advanced software, we aim to transform healthcare delivery, enabling clinicians to prioritize patient care over administrative tasks.

At Harchive, we believe in harnessing technology to make a tangible difference in the lives of patients and healthcare providers alike. Join us on our journey to revolutionize healthcare in Nigeria.
